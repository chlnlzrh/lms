## RISK MITIGATION

### Analysis Accuracy Risk
- **Mitigation**: Rigorous validation of assumptions and conclusions
- **Control**: Peer review of analyses, stakeholder validation
- **Monitoring**: Comparison of forecast vs. actual, lessons learned tracking

### Scope Creep Risk
- **Mitigation**: Clear scope definition and change control process
- **Control**: Regular scope review and stakeholder alignment
- **Monitoring**: Scope tracking and change history analysis

### Stakeholder Alignment Risk
- **Mitigation**: Early and frequent stakeholder engagement
- **Control**: Regular communication and feedback incorporation
- **Monitoring**: Stakeholder satisfaction surveys and alignment assessment

### Solution Design Risk
- **Mitigation**: Thorough requirement analysis and solution validation
- **Control**: Design review and stakeholder approval
- **Monitoring**: Post-implementation review and actual vs. expected

### Implementation Risk
- **Mitigation**: Comprehensive change management and adoption planning
- **Control**: Adoption tracking and support provision
- **Monitoring**: Adoption metrics and benefit realization tracking
