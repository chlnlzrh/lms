## RISK MITIGATION

### Schedule Risk
- **Mitigation**: Conservative estimates with contingency buffers
- **Control**: Weekly schedule reviews and early warning systems
- **Monitoring**: Schedule variance tracking and trend analysis

### Budget Risk
- **Mitigation**: Detailed bottom-up budgeting with contingencies
- **Control**: Weekly budget tracking and variance analysis
- **Monitoring**: Quarterly budget forecast updates

### Scope Creep Risk
- **Mitigation**: Detailed scope definition and change control process
- **Control**: Regular scope review and strict change management
- **Monitoring**: Scope tracking and change history analysis

### Quality Risk
- **Mitigation**: Defined quality standards and testing procedures
- **Control**: Peer review and acceptance criteria verification
- **Monitoring**: Defect tracking and rework rate analysis

### Team Risk
- **Mitigation**: Early skill assessment and capability gaps
- **Control**: Training and mentoring as needed
- **Monitoring**: Team capability tracking and performance reviews

### Client Risk
- **Mitigation**: Clear communication and expectation management
- **Control**: Regular communication and issue escalation
- **Monitoring**: Client satisfaction surveys and feedback
